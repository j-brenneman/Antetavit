    Multiple issues with this product
    By Philip Oliver on August 15, 2013
    Size: SmallColor: Ash Heather Verified Purchase
    The product shipped and arrived in a timely manner, as Amazon is usually fantastic on this issue.

    This tank top will run at least one full size larger than most other manufacturers. I bought the Ash Heather color. There are several stripes of white on the product image on the site, but what I received is black or grey in all of those places. In other words, the colors are completely different than what they were advertising. Since I bought Ash Heather, they have apparently discontinued this color.

    The tank is of great fabric quality, and I am sure that it will last a long time. There is fantastic craftsmanship here.

    In conclusion, I think LRG has a great product here, it's just something that you should buy from a B&M store. They just haven't figured out the nuances of selling online, in terms of accurate coloring, or providing a sizing chart. If I had the chance to go back, I would NOT have ordered this product.
